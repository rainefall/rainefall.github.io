Hello, my name is Riley and I am a programmer and independent musician. I have been programming for longer than I remember, though I regard 2015 as the year when I would begin to describe myself as someone who actually knows what they’re doing. I have been using C# since 2014, Ruby since 2017 and C/C++ since 2018.

In my time programming I have written many video games, some of which can be found here, others here. I have experience in the Unity, Godot and GameMaker:Studio game engines, and briefly worked with Unity in a semi-professional capacity in 2022 as a side project for a software company I was working for at the time. I have also attempted to write my own game engines/frameworks using OpenGL in the past.

As mentioned previously I am also a musician, I have released numerous remixes/covers of popular video game soundtracks under the name rainefall, though my main musical output comes in the form of my synthwave/alternative rock project “Dream Library”. Under this name I have released 1 double LP length album, and two EPs (one original, one cover EP of other electronic/alt rock songs). More info on this can be found here

My musical output also extends to original video game music, I have written music for both my own projects and various independent/hobbyist projects that I have come into contact with via the internet. More info on this can be found here.
