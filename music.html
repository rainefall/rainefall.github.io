dream library

Dream Library is a musical project that began in 2018 as an output for synthwave/retrowave leaning music. Over the course of the 4 years it took me to write, record, produce and release the first Dream Library album, that style changed drastically into an "alternative rock solo act with synthwave/electronica roots". I have further albums planned, in which I plan to delve into styles such as progressive rock/metal, post punk and breakcore.
